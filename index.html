Frequently Asked Questions

Got Questions?

What services does Sri Medha Infra provide?
Sri Medha Infra offers comprehensive construction and material supply services including Construction Services, Material Supply, Equipment Rental, Project Management, Maintenance & Support, and Technical Consulting. We cater to projects of all scales with professional expertise.
How do you ensure timely delivery of materials?
We maintain a dedicated logistics team and modern transportation fleet to ensure timely delivery. Each order is tracked in real-time, and we coordinate closely with our clients to meet project deadlines without compromise.
What equipment is available for rent?
We have 185+ equipment units available including Tower Cranes, JCBs, Excavators, Concrete Millers, Vibro Rollers, Scaffolding, and various other specialized machinery. All equipment undergoes regular maintenance to ensure optimal performance.
How many projects have you completed?
Sri Medha Infra has successfully completed 100+ projects for 13+ satisfied clients. Our portfolio includes residential, commercial, and industrial construction projects of varying complexity and scale.
Do you handle emergency orders?
Yes, we understand that construction projects sometimes require urgent material supply or equipment. We maintain a rapid response team to handle emergency orders and expedite delivery whenever needed.
What is your experience with large projects?
Our team of 50+ skilled professionals has extensive experience handling projects of all scales. We have successfully managed complex construction projects with tight timelines and high-quality standards for leading industry clients.
Do you provide project management services?
Yes, we offer comprehensive project management services including planning, budget management, timeline coordination, quality assurance, and resource allocation to ensure your project runs smoothly from start to finish.
How do you ensure quality of materials and services?
All materials are sourced from reputable suppliers and undergo rigorous quality checks. Our Quality Lead oversees every aspect, ensuring materials meet industry standards and customer specifications consistently.
What are your payment terms?
We offer flexible payment terms tailored to your project requirements. Options include upfront payment, installment plans, and credit terms for established clients. Please contact us for customized payment solutions.
Do you offer after-sales support?
Absolutely! We provide comprehensive maintenance and support services to ensure your equipment remains in optimal condition. Our maintenance team is available for regular servicing and emergency repairs.
